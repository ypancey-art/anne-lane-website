European-Trained Naturopath · TCM · Functional & Energy Medicine

If you've landed here, you're probably looking for a solution to a specific problem — something ongoing that you just can't seem to get a handle on, even after trying all the things. That's often where I come into the picture.

As a European-trained Naturopath, I integrate Traditional Chinese Medicine, Functional Medicine, and Energy Medicine to support you on your path to feeling healthy and vital again. Most of my work has been with women going through perimenopause, as well as couples who want to conceive. Getting to the root cause of hormonal imbalances, nutritional deficiencies, and gut issues is at the heart of everything I do — whether I'm working with women or men.

I've developed a framework that allows me to hone in on the main issues your body is struggling with and shift the energy quickly. Our initial 90-minute session reveals an outline for working together — and often comes with a list of "homework" we follow up on in subsequent sessions. That's why it's best if we work together over a minimum of 3 months, so I can truly support you in meeting your goals.

On a personal note: I'm a Gen Xer (and not a fan of social media), a mom to two teenage superdudes, a 25-year expat, traveller, reader, and dedicated to creating a healthier future generation. I love working with people who are ready to do the work.
