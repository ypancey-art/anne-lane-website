Perimenopause Support

Perimenopause can feel like your body has a mind of its own — and conventional medicine often doesn't have satisfying answers. I get to the root cause of your hormonal shifts using an integrated blend of TCM, Functional Medicine, and Energy Medicine.

Fertility Support

  • Couples-based naturopathic care
